Cooked Indian Bread

20 Minutes - 1 Portion

Ingredients

  • - Indian Bread (Ideally a day old): 1-2
  • - Garlic: 3-4
  • - Ginger: 1
  • - Green Chilli Pepper: 1-2
  • - Onion: 1-2
  • - Curry Leaves (Optional): 4-5
  • - Turmeric Powder: Pinch
  • - Coriander Powder: 1 Tbsp
  • - Red Chilli Powder: 1 - 1 1/2 Tbsp
  • - Asafoetida Powder: Pinch (Use Carefully)
  • - Garam Masala: 2-3 Pinches
  • - Jaggery: 1 Tbsp
  • - Mango Powder: 1/2 Tbsp
  • - Cooking Oil
